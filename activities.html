Top 5 Christmas Activities For Families

1. Take a Family Holiday Card Photo


Christmas is fast approaching! Plan ahead for a family photoshoot and get your family Christmas card ready in advance.

2. Visit A Christmas Tree Farm


Holiday season is about creating memories. It's a fun Christmas family activity to visit a tree farm and be able to pick the perfect tree for Christmas!

3.Decorate A Gingerbread House


Nothing beats an activity that involves decorating and being able to eat it afterwards. Make it fun and festive!

4. Holiday Family Game Night


I'm always looking forward to family game nights! Play rounds of board games, Charades, Pictionary, Brain Teasers over Winter break or Family Christmas parties. It's so fun!

5. Watch Christmas Movies


Christmas is not Christmas if there's no movie watching. Spend time with your family watching these all time favorite movies from our list:
